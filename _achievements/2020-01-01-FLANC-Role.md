Elected and served a distinguished two-year term as the **Delegada de Residentes de Ecuador** representing the country within the *Federación Latinoamericana de Sociedades de Neurocirugía (FLANC)*. 

This role involved significant coordination, leadership, and advocacy for training standards and educational opportunities across Latin American neurosurgical resident programs.
