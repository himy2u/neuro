**Situation:** The rapid advancement of neurosurgical techniques requires standardized, high-quality training protocols across diverse and developing healthcare ecosystems in Latin America.

**Action:** Elected to serve a distinguished two-year term as the **Delegada de Residentes de Ecuador** representing the country within the *Federación Latinoamericana de Sociedades de Neurocirugía (FLANC)*. Demonstrated rigorous professional leadership by representing the clinical, technical, and educational interests of neurosurgical residents spanning multiple Latin American nations.

**Result:** Successfully advocated for elevated training standards, formalized critical educational frameworks, and established robust international collaboration pathways that directly improved the competency metrics of international surgical cohorts.
